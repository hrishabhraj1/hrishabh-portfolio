Founder Content Project

Client Australian Startup Founder
Role Video Editor & Hook Strategist
Services Script Doctoring, Editing, Retention Design
Timeline 6 Months (Ongoing)

Overview

Collaborated with an Australian technology founder to transform complex technical insights into highly engaging, structured short-form videos. The core objective was to drive audience retention and position the founder as a clear voice in startup development.

The Challenge

The founder possessed deep industry knowledge but struggled to package it for short-form video platforms. Pacing was slow, ideas were overly academic, and audiences were dropping off within the first 3 seconds of playback.

The Strategy

We re-engineered the content format. Instead of generic introductions, we structured hooks around immediate industry tension. The editing language was designed to be clean and minimal, avoiding typical noisy trends. We focused on clear visual pacing, subtle text support, and natural line breaks to maximize readability and information retention.

The Execution

Applied precise cuts to eliminate unnecessary pauses while preserving the founder's conversational authenticity. Clean typography layouts were used for metadata and keywords, emphasizing core points without distracting from the speaker. The system led to a 3x increase in video completion rates, building an engaged audience of founders, builders, and potential clients.
