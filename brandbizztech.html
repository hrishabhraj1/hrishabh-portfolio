BrandBizzTech

Client Digital Marketing Agency
Role Content Strategist & Video Editor
Services Strategy, Design Systems, Editing
Timeline 4 Months

Overview

BrandBizzTech, a digital marketing agency, needed to establish authority and attract high-tier clients. The goal was to build a personal branding system for the agency leadership, transforming complex marketing concepts into engaging, digestible content.

The Challenge

The agency was generating significant results for clients but struggled to maintain their own content pipeline. Their video content was irregular, lacked visual cohesion, and failed to reflect the premium nature of their services.

The Strategy

We established a content system designed to communicate marketing expertise. The core approach centered on educational hooks, transparent breakdowns of active agency campaigns, and high-retention visual editing. By stripping away generic motion graphics and adopting a clean, typographic style, the videos stood out for their content depth rather than visual noise.

The Execution

Implemented a batch-production model and styled templates with distinct typography (clean, bold san-serif headers and dark background settings). Each frame was optimized for mobile consumption, prioritizing text readability and smooth pacing. This workflow enabled the agency to scale content output from one video per fortnight to four weekly pieces, resulting in a consistent rise in inbound consultation requests.
